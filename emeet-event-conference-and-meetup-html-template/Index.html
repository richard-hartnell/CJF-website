Cascade Jugglers is the Seattle-area International Jugglers' Association (IJA) affiliate club, named for the Cascade mountain range that lies to the east of Seattle (and the most basic of juggling patterns). Jugglers of all ability-levels are welcome.

Seattle Juggling Festival is May 24th-26th 2019

Meetings

The Cascade Jugglers meet on:
  • Wednesday evenings 6:30pm until 8:30pm at Ballard Community Center, at 6020 28th Ave NW. Half of the gym is for juggling (the other half is pickle ball players). Be sure to check with the front desk, you may need to sign in.

There are a number of other groups that meet in the Puget Sound area that you may be interested in;

  • The Bothell Jugglers meet on Wednesday at 7pm W.A. Anderson Gym has a Juggle 4 Fun class or Wilmot Gateway Park during the summer. Check their website for more details.
  • The Bellingham Circus Guild has a group of jugglers that meet Monday nights. Please contact them for details
