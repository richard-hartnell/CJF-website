The Cascade Juggling Festival

The Bellingham Circus Guild and The Cascade Jugglers

Present

Cascade Juggling Festival 2024

Friday & Sunday: Bellingham Circus Guild, 1401 6th St

Saturday: Fairhaven Middle School Gymnasium, 110 Parkridge Rd

The first annual Cascade Juggling Festival is coming to Bellingham!!


Come juggle, spin, flow your way to our gathering of prop-manipulating circus artists from the Salish Sea and beyond. All-levels, all-ages workshops to be offered throughout the weekend with balls, clubs, rings, hoops, poi, staff, and other props, as well as related art forms like flexibility, dance, and performing. For spectators, an invitational revue will feature top-notch talent on Saturday evening in a not-to-be-missed variety show!

PROGRAMMING:

Friday: open gym at the Bellingham Circus Guild; glow jam feat. The Velveteen DJ and DJ Parts Per Million
Saturday: open gym at the Fairhaven Middle School gymnasium, followed by an all-star gala show at the Bellingham Circus Guild on Saturday eve
Sunday: open gym at the Bellingham Circus Guild with jugglers' games and the presentation of the Della Plaster award

PARKING:

Parking is free and abundant! (please carpool.)

PRICING:

Gym and workshops are pay-what-you can, $0 to $999! No-one turned away for lack of funds.
Gala show tickets $25 and up.

FOOD:

There are many options for food, whether supermarket-style or restaurant-and-cafe-style, within a handful of blocks.

HOUSING:

If you are coming from out of town, please reach out early to find local circus people to stay with! There's heaps of us! There is also a variety of charming stays available nearby for rent.

ETC:

In the city "where the Cascades reach the sea," Bellingham has an impressive array of beautiful hikes, bikes, and other local to-dos. And if you're sticking around after, the weekend following is Northwest Folklife, a famous destination for buskers.
All event locations are accessible. The gala show will run a 21+ bar but the venue is all-ages.
